Our range of services & products at Jaytech Fire Security Systems


Fire Fighting Equipment

We provide a range of portable fire fighting equipment of different types; dry powder, water, foam and carbon dioxide extinguishers. They are inconformity with BS EN3 and NFPA standards.

Closed Circuit Television Systems(CCTV's)

We install and commission CCTV systems for security purposes which capture and record minute by minute daily occurrences in 24 hrs and can easily be retrieved on client’s quest

Fire Alarm Systems

We install both addressable and convectional fire alarm systems. The intelligent systems are integrated with controls to provide solutions for large & medium sized projects. Convectional alarm system can suit small sized projects .i.e. offices, households
